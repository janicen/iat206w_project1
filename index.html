Despite being designed as a patriotic soldier for the United State of America, Captain America has gained popularity amongst many countries outside of America. According to boxofficemojo.com the 2014 blockbuster “Captain America: The Winter Soldier” made over $455 million from foreign grosses, making up 63% of its total gross. We see an opportunity to localize Captain America for top grossing countries to make him culturally relevant. This could be changing the existing aspects of Captain America, adding to the dynamics of the character or creating a whole new character inspired by the heroic archetypes that defines Captain America. We’ve selected four top grossing countries: Mexico, Brazil, United Kingdom and Russia as target cultures. woot woot
